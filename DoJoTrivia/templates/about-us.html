About Us



DojoTrivia, starting out as a school project assigned to four college students, turned out to be a lot of work. We had four weeks time, which we thought would be more than enough, but we guessed wrong. In those four weeks, a lot of time was spent creating this very website, implementing all necessary functions. For one of the students, it was his first time working with HTML (except for the other assignments in the first week). This wouldn't matter, however, as it turned out to be a huge amount of work, with great difficulty. Multiple languages were combined in the making of DoJoTrivia, including: Python, HTML, CSS, SQL, and eventually JavaScript. Combining all of these wasn't an easy task either. Loads of time was spent Googling questions and looking at tutorials.



And here we are, proud to present to you: DoJoTrivia, a place where you can test your knowledge and of challenge your friends! We hope you enjoy!



Sincerely,

The DoJoTrivia Team
